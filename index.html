What is it (Other Than a Colour?)

Lavender is loosely based off of Jade , created by the founders of the Daft Labs team to bring peace and order to their office. Lavender is a templating engine with further simplified syntax to allow for ease of use (as well as less time spent double checking for proper useage).

This project is maintained on GitHub, allowing users to collaborate and further building.
