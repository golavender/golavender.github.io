About Us

Lavender was formed in the pursuit of simplicity and ease. The syntax is faster and easier to type and works like Jade. If you feel like it, why not help us make a difference?

The Name

When naming Lavender, we took inspiration from Jade itself. Jade is three things: a color, an item and a programming language. We also had help from JT, the co-founder of daft labs, whose favorite color is purple. It was his wife who made the connection between the name Lavender and the budding programming language, and thus the name stuck.
